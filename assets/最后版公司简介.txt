中文版（官网正式版）

石家庄信邦粘合剂有限公司（QLHB）成立于2000年，注册资本300万元人民币，坐落于河北省省会石家庄市。公司是一家专注于环保型水性粘合剂研发、生产及系统化应用解决方案的国家级高新技术企业。

公司建有完善的水性粘合剂研发中心、中试基地及规模化生产基地，长期聚焦于水性树脂、水性丙烯酸体系及水性聚氨酯体系的技术研发与产业化应用，面向以纸制品包装为核心的材料复合行业提供稳定、高效、环保的整体粘接解决方案。

信邦始终坚持以自主研发与持续技术创新为核心发展战略，累计投入超过3000万元人民币用于前期产品研发及产学研合作。公司在水性胶粘剂领域与北京化工大学、北京理工大学、华南理工大学等国内知名高校建立了长期、深入的科研合作关系，在配方设计、性能优化及检测验证等方面形成了坚实的技术支撑体系。

公司研发中心配备了系统化、专业化的胶粘剂检测与分析设备，包括微机控制电子万能材料试验机、高低温湿热试验箱、T 系列触摸屏粘度计、DASP 阻尼系统等，可对产品的物理性能、环境适应性及应用稳定性进行全流程、实时检测，为产品规模化投产及质量一致性提供可靠保障。

依托成熟的配方平台与工程化能力，信邦以技术与配方输出为市场切入点，现已服务30 余个细分应用领域，涵盖数百种产品型号，并可根据客户的实际应用场景、材料结构及工艺条件，专业定制各类特殊用途胶粘剂解决方案。

截至目前，公司已获得国家发明专利 5 项、实用新型专利 1 项，并通过 ISO 9001 质量管理体系认证及 IATF 16949 质量管理体系认证。产品符合 SGS、RoHS、REACH 等国际标准，同时满足 GB 18583、HJ-2541 等中国环保法规要求。公司荣获河北省“专精特新”企业称号。

在可持续发展方面，信邦积极履行对员工、客户及环境的社会责任，连续多年通过 CSR 社会责任认证，并获得 EcoVadis 银牌认证，持续推动企业在环境、社会与治理（ESG）领域的高质量发展。

English Version (Official Website Copy)

Shijiazhuang Xinbang Adhesives Co., Ltd. (QLHB) was founded in 2000 with a registered capital of RMB 3 million. Located in Shijiazhuang, the capital city of Hebei Province, China, the company is a national high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, manufacturing, and application-oriented solutions of environmentally friendly water-based adhesives.

QLHB operates an integrated system consisting of a water-based adhesive R&D center, pilot-scale facilities, and large-scale production bases. The company focuses on the development and industrial application of water-based resin systems, water-based acrylic systems, and water-based polyurethane systems, providing stable, efficient, and sustainable bonding solutions primarily for the paper packaging and material lamination industries.

Driven by a long-term commitment to independent innovation and continuous technological advancement, the company has invested more than RMB 30 million in early-stage product development and industry–university research collaborations. In the field of water-based adhesive technology, QLHB maintains close and long-term cooperation with leading institutions such as Beijing University of Chemical Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology, and South China University of Technology, forming a solid technical foundation in formulation design, performance optimization, and testing validation.

The company’s R&D center is equipped with a comprehensive range of professional adhesive testing and analytical instruments, including computerized universal testing machines, high- and low-temperature humidity chambers, T-series touchscreen viscometers, and DASP damping systems. These facilities enable full-process and real-time evaluation of product performance, ensuring consistent quality and reliable performance in large-scale production.

Leveraging its mature formulation platforms and engineering expertise, QLHB adopts a technology- and formulation-driven approach as its market strategy. The company has served over 30 application segments and developed hundreds of product models, while also providing professionally engineered, customized specialty adhesive solutions tailored to customers’ specific application requirements, material structures, and process conditions.

To date, the company holds five national invention patents and one utility model patent, and has obtained ISO 9001 and IATF 16949 quality management system certifications. Its products comply with SGS, RoHS, and REACH standards, as well as relevant Chinese environmental regulations including GB 18583 and HJ-2541. QLHB has also been recognized as a “Specialized, Sophisticated, and Innovative” enterprise by the Hebei Provincial Government.

Committed to sustainable development, QLHB actively fulfills its social responsibilities toward employees, customers, and the environment. The company has passed CSR certifications for multiple consecutive years and has been awarded the EcoVadis Silver Medal, demonstrating its strong and long-term commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) excellence.
