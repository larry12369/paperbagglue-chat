Press right after gluing: At least 10KG of pressure for 15+ minutes. No rebound or uneven pressing—otherwise it won’t stick. Sealant stays sticky if not pressed—that’s normal! More pressure + longer pressing = better penetration (industry standard: 10KG for 15+ minutes).
